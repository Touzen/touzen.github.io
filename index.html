Thomas Vakili is a PhD student at Stockholm University where he researches Natural Language Processing. He is a KTH alumnus and previously worked as a back-end developer and data engineer.

Setting the tech stuff aside, he has also been politically involved for over a decade in various roles in the struggle for social democracy. Nowadays, he mostly rants about politics.

As I mentioned at the top, I am a passionate socialist and sometimes reflect on what must be done on my political blog. Most of the text is in Swedish, and not all of it is strictly political. I also collect other things I have written, for example for the student union magazine Osqledaren.

I try to collect useful tips & tricks on my writing blog. I also document whenever I deep-dive into interesting projects, both related to my research and when I come up with exciting side-projects.
